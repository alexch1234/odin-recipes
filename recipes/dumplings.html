Dumplings

Description

Recipe for dumplings. Recipe inspired by Joshua Weissman

Ingredients

  • 450g ground meat
  • 3 cabbage leaves, finely chopped
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 inch cube ginger, grated or very finely chopped
  • 3 spring onions, finely choppede
  • 3g sugar
  • 2g salt
  • 1tbs (14g) soy sauce
  • 1tsp sesame oil

Steps

  1. To create the filling, add all the ingredients into a large bowl and knead for around 2 minutes until homogenised.
  2. Let the filling reset in the fridge overnight
  3. Pack the filling into your dumpling wrappers
  4. Steam for 8 minutes to cooks

Tips for packing dumplings:

  1. Pinch the middle together like a taco
  2. Going left or right, fold one side towards the middle and pinch to seal. Repeat with the other side.
